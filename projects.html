Encrypt0

Encrypt0 was a project developed for Hello World Hackathon at Purdue University. It is a simple encrypting and decrypting Android App, that uses a cryptography algorithm developed by the team during the event.

Collaboration with shivap98, kartikmittal33 and muriloogps;

SDL Breakout

SDL Breakout was a project developed as a final project for Computing I at UFRJ, implemented in C using SDL2. It is a simple yet fun Breakout game.
