About Us

Scope Consulting is a non-profit organization formed and managed by undergraduate students from Purdue University (West Lafayette, IN). We are affiliated to the Junior Enterprise movement, and our goal is to bring innovations and quality to businesses near us through engineering and software consulting projects, while improving the technical and professional skills of our members.

The Junior Enterprise Movement

A movement with 30,000 members across two dozen countries, encouraging members to develop an enterpreneur spirit and developing their students professionally and personally.
